About Us

Our Mission

St. John's Indian Orthodox Church is a vibrant spiritual community that honors the rich traditions of the Orthodox faith while embracing contemporary ways of serving God and our community.

We believe in creating a welcoming space where everyone can encounter the divine, grow in faith, and find meaningful connections with others who share the journey.
