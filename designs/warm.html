About Us

Rooted in tradition, growing in faith

St John's Indian Orthodox Church of Delaware Valley is a vibrant spiritual community dedicated to preserving the rich traditions of the Orthodox faith while serving our community with love and devotion.

We welcome all who seek to grow in faith, experience the beauty of Orthodox worship, and find a spiritual home where tradition meets fellowship. Whether you're a lifelong Orthodox Christian or exploring the faith for the first time, you'll find a warm welcome here.
